1. Organisation Overview: 
❖ NAME: Modon Tech 
❖ FOUNDING AND EARLY YEARS: FOUNDING AND EARLY YEARS: Modon was cofounded by 
Motheo Banda and Liyanda Banda in Johannesburg South Africa in 1937 originally under the 
name SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT LABORATORIES (SDL)  
❖ NAME CHANGES AND GROWTH: In 1950, the company changed its name to Modon 
database. The company then went public in 1970 and by 1971, the company had become 
one of the biggest tech companies of the generation and continues to strive 
❖ MAJOR DEVELOPMENTS AND ACQUISTIONS: Through the 1990’s and 2000’s Modon 
expanded its product and market presence through aggressive acquisitions. Big 
acquisitions that they acquired are PEOPLESOFT in 2005, SIEBEL SYSTEMS in 2007 and BEA 
SYSTEMS in 2008. 
❖ RECENT INNOVATIONS: In recent years, Modon Tech has focused on cloud computing and 
artificial intelligence, launching products like the Modon Autonomous Database, which 
automates database management tasks. The company continues to be a major in the 
technology sector, consistently ranking among the top software companies globally. 
❖ MISSION STATEMENT: To help people see data in new ways, discover insights, and unlock 
endless possibilities. Modon Tech aims to provide tools and platforms that enable users to 
visualize and understand data more effectively. 
❖ VISION STATEMENT: Empower oranisation to innovate and achieve sustainable growth 
through advanced technology solutions. Modon Tech aims to keep its top position in the 
technology sector. 
❖ TARGET AUDICENCE: The target audience for Modon tech is; Enterprise-level Businesses, 
Decision makers, Functional owners, Developers and Database Administrators . 
2. Website Goals and Objectives:  
The purpose of this website is to provide useful information about the 
technological products of Modon Tech. It will have the products pictures and 
information about the product next to it so that users know what they are buying. 
KPI’s: 
Customer Satisfaction: Reviews, ratings, and feedback from customers. 
Order Delivery Success Rate: Percentage of orders delivered on time. 
Page load time: How fast the website loads (affects user experience). 
Cart Abandonment Rate: Percentage of users who add items to cart but do not 
complete the purchase. 
3. Current Website Analysis (if applicable): 
▪ Strengths: The current Modon Tech website allows users to view available 
products, see the company’s address, and contact the organization easily. The 
layout is simple and functional, providing essential information to visitors. 
▪ Weaknesses: The website currently lacks product pricing and detailed descriptions 
of how each product works. It also has limited visual content, which can make it 
harder for users to fully understand what they are purchasing. 
▪ Opportunities: Adding more product images and detailed specifications will 
improve user engagement and trust. Including pricing information and interactive 
elements such as a product comparison or demo section will make the site more 
informative and user-friendly. 
▪ User Experience Feedback: While navigation is straightforward, the overall design 
could be modernized to align with Modon Tech’s technological brand identity. 
Enhancing responsiveness and page load speed will further improve the user 
experience. 
4. Proposed Website Features and Functionality: 
• Homepage: The homepage will introduce Modon Tech and show its main products 
and services. It will include a navigation menu, featured product images, and a brief 
company overview to engage visitors immediately. 
• About Us Page: This page will provide detailed information about Modon Tech’s 
history, mission, vision, and founders. It will help users understand the company’s 
background and credibility in the technology industry. 
• Services Page: The services page will outline the many technological solutions 
offered by Modon Tech, including cloud computing, database management, and AI 
tools. Each service will have a short description and relevant visuals. 
• News Page: The news section will display updates about Modon Tech’s latest 
innovations, product launches, and industry developments. It will keep users 
informed and demonstrate the company’s ongoing growth. 
• Contact Page: This page will include contact details, a physical address, and a 
contact form for inquiries. It will also feature links to social media platforms for 
easy communication. 
• Product Display Feature: Each product will have an image and description next to 
it, allowing users to understand what they are purchasing. This feature enhances 
transparency and user confidence. 
• Search and Navigation: A simple and efficient navigation bar will allow users to 
move easily between pages. A search function will help users find specific products 
or information quickly. 
• Responsive Design: The website will be optimized for all devices — desktop, 
tablet, and mobile — ensuring accessibility and a smooth user experience. 
5. Design and User Experience: 
• Overall Design Aesthetic: The website will have a modern and professional look 
that reflects Modon Tech’s identity as a technology company. The design will be 
simple, clean, and easy to navigate, ensuring users can quickly find information 
about products and services. 
• Color Palette: The primary colors will be dark blue and grey, representing trust, 
innovation, and professionalism. These colors complement the technological 
theme and create a sleek, corporate appearance. 
• Typography: The font style will be clear and readable. The business name will 
appear in a larger, bold font to stand out, while paragraph text will be smaller and 
consistent for readability. Headings will be distinct to guide users through each 
section. 
• Layout and Navigation: The website will feature a simple and efficient layout with 
clearly labeled pages — Home, About Us, Services, News, and Contact. Navigation 
menus will be intuitive, allowing users to move easily between sections. 
• User Experience: The design will focus on accessibility and responsiveness. All 
pages will load quickly and adjust seamlessly to different screen sizes (desktop, 
tablet, and mobile). Buttons and links will be clearly visible and easy to interact 
with. 
• Visual Elements: Product images will be displayed alongside descriptions to help 
users understand what they are purchasing. Consistent spacing and alignment will 
maintain a professional appearance throughout the site.

6. Technical Requirements: 
▪ Programming Languages: The website will be developed using HTML, CSS, and 
JavaScript. 
o HTML will structure the content and layout of the pages. 
o CSS will handle the visual design, including colors, fonts, and spacing. 
o JavaScript will add interactivity, such as dynamic menus or form validation. 
▪ Hosting and Domain Requirements: The site will require a domain name (e.g., 
modontech.co.za) and reliable hosting to ensure accessibility and uptime. Hosting 
should support modern web standards and SSL security. 
▪ Browser Compatibility: The website will be optimized for major browsers including 
Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Firefox, and Safari to ensure consistent user 
experience. 
▪ Responsive Design: The website will be fully responsive, adapting to different 
screen sizes such as desktops, tablets, and mobile devices. 
▪ Security Requirements: SSL encryption will be implemented to protect user data 
and ensure secure communication between the server and users. 
▪ Maintenance Tools: Basic tools for updates, backups, and analytics (e.g., Google 
Analytics) will be integrated to monitor performance and user engagement. 
7. Timeline and Milestones: 
• Week 1 – Planning and Initial Design: Finalize the website structure (homepage, 
about, contact, services, and news pages). Create wireframes and select the color 
palette (dark blue and grey). Begin coding the basic layout using HTML and CSS. 
• Week 2 – Development Phase: Implement interactive elements using JavaScript. 
Add product information and images. Test navigation and responsiveness across 
different devices to ensure a smooth user experience. 
• Week 3 – Testing and Final Touches: Conduct usability testing and fix any bugs. 
Optimize page load speed and verify that all links and forms function correctly. 
Perform a final review and deploy the website. 
• Estimated Completion Time: The project will take approximately 2–3 weeks to 
complete, with final delivery expected by the end of Week 3 (around 15 working 
days). 
• Notes: Minor adjustments and content updates may continue after launch. The 
timeline assumes all product images and descriptions are available at the start of 
development. If additional features such as e-commerce or AI integration are added 
later, allow 1–2 extra weeks for development.
